International version of Sorteria

Sorteria’s vision goes beyond Norway’s borders

The idea is that the concept should first be tested in Norway to get an overview of the interest and impact of the product. This is because Norway's effort is ranked as 5th in the world where 40% of total waste is recycled. It is reasonable to assume that the focus towards climate friendly actions will increase in the future. It is therefore important that the tools that are developed make these solutions accessible to everyone.

Sorteria needs to be inclusive and adaptable

In order to best implement this solution in other countries where the technology landscape is different. When sharing this solution with other countries, considering the widespread usage of phones is crucial.

Sorteria’s features are relevant to an international target

This will require knowledge related to each country’s rules on recycling and waste management to allow the app to achieve international accessibility.
